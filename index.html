WristNav

Full Wear OS app for hiking, running and cycling. Offline OSM maps on your wrist, GPX route navigation, GPS recording that keeps going when the screen sleeps, live pace / HR / elevation, auto-pause, lap splits, Strava export. Companion phone app to plan routes, push GPX to the watch and receive recorded tracks.
